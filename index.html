U
Why does my model keep overfitting no matter what I try?
◈
Great question! Overfitting usually means memorizing training data. Check: How big is your training set vs model? Are you using dropout or weight decay? 💡
U
I'm not using dropout. Should I add it?
◈
Dropout is one of the most effective tools here! It randomly "turns off" neurons during training. Build AI Lesson 5 covers this in depth — start there and come back with questions 🎯
