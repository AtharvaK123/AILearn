Why does my model keep overfitting no matter what I try?
◈
Overfitting means your model is memorizing training data instead of generalizing. A few things to check: How does your training set size compare to model complexity? Are you using dropout or weight decay? What does your validation curve look like? 💡
U
I'm not using dropout at all. Should I add it?
◈
Yes! Dropout randomly deactivates neurons during training, forcing the network to learn redundant representations. It's one of the most effective regularization tools we have. The Deep Learning academy covers this in detail — start there and come back with questions 🎯
